About Us

Our concept revolves around agricultural education that promotes organic farming practices in India. This innovative approach combines experiential learning, eco-tourism, and organic farming to create a unique and valuable experience.Visitors, including students and tourists , will actively participate in various agricultural activities. They'll gain hands-on experience in organic farming methods, such as composting, crop rotation, and pest control without chemicals. This not only educates them about the importance of sustainable agriculture but also fosters a deeper connection to the environment.Farmers will benefit from this initiative through multiple income streams. They can charge for tours, workshops, and farm produce, diversifying their revenue sources. Additionally, the knowledge-sharing aspect enables farmers to earn consulting fees, transforming them into experts in organic farming. This holistic approach aligns with India's growing focus on eco-friendly practices, making it a promising venture with both social and economic benefits.
